Welcome to the SwimPlace support page. Here, you'll find answers to frequently asked questions, information on how to get assistance with the app, and an overview of its functionality.

App Functionality

With SwimPlace, you can:

  • Create session plans tailored to your swimming routine.
  • Save and manage your plans for future reference.
  • Add your personal times to monitor your progress.
  • View club events, a feature especially useful if you're a member of a swimming club.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I install SwimPlace?
A: Download SwimPlace from the App Store and follow the on-screen installation instructions.

Q: How do I report a bug or issue?
A: If you encounter any problems while using SwimPlace, please email us at tom@swimplan.co.uk with a detailed description of the issue.
