Roadmap

Last updated: 1st December 2023

This Roadmap outlines the planned phases for the development and enhancement of the SwimPlace mobile application. Our goal is to continuously improve our app to better serve our users.

Phase 1: Let's Get Started (Complete)

  • Develop a feature to automatically create swimming session plans.
  • Implement functionality to display swimming sessions.
  • Implement functionality to let people know where and when swimming sessions are.
  • Enable storing user time data for performance tracking.
  • Incorporate a news section to keep users informed about swimming-related news.
